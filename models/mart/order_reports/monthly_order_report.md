{% docs monthly_order_report %}

This model creates a monthly summary of orders, includes the following:
- ** TOTAL NUMBER OF ORDERS:** Total number of orders 
- ** TOTAL DISTINCT PRODUCTS ORDERED:** Total individual products ordered
- ** TOTAL NUMBER OF PRODUCTS PURCHASED:** Total number of products ordered
- ** AVERAGE ORDER DISCOUNT: ** Average discount on orders that month
- ** TOTAL ORDER AMOUNT:** Total amount on orders that month

This allows for monthly sales performance analysis.
